Phong Components Breakdown

AMBIENT

Ia = Ka × Ia_light
Constant base light

DIFFUSE

Id = Kd × (N·L)
Lambert shading

SPECULAR

Is = Ks × (R·V)^α
Shiny highlight

Phong Illumination Model:
I = Ka·Ia + Kd·(N·L)·Id + Ks·(R·V)^α·Is

N = surface normal (normalized)
L = direction to light (normalized)
V = direction to viewer (normalized)
R = 2(N·L)N - L (reflection vector)
α = shininess exponent

Ka=ambient Kd=diffuse Ks=specular
